Meet Dr. Liping Su, General Dentist

Your trusted dentist at Thorough Dental Centre

Dr Liping Su is an experienced, knowledgeable and passionate dentist who graduated with a Bachelor of Dental Surgery from the University of Adelaide, one of the finest dental school's in the country. She furthered her formal dental education with the completion of a Post Graduate Diploma of Dental Implantology at Charles Sturt University, where she received the Dean's Award for Academic Excellence during the course of her study.

Dr Su has worked in both private and public dental practices for over two decades, and enjoys all aspects of dentistry, particularly on attaining excellent oral health and creating optimal aesthetics. She approaches all her work with consideration of her patient's best interests. She also ensures all her patients understand their available treatment options so they can choose the most appropriate care for themselves. With a gentle and understanding nature , all her patients are comfortable and at ease during their visits, including children and anxious patients.

Dr Su is a certified Invisalign provider since 2014. She recently completed a Post Graduate study for Mastering Aligning as well as furthering education in the area of dental implants, endodontics, paediatric dentistry and sleep medicine. So you can rest assure that you are in capable hands when you come to Thorough Dental Care.
