Looking for a specific general dental treatment?

Thorough Dental is proud to be a one-stop shop for all your routine dental care. From annual cleanings and comprehensive exams to preventive treatments like fillings, sealants, and gum disease therapy, we have everything you need to bring out your best smile.

Our experienced team provides gentle, thorough care using modern techniques and technologies to ensure your comfort and long-term oral health.

Dental Cleanings

Cleaning and exam visits are comfortable and designed to maintain the health of your teeth and gums. Visit us at least twice a year to fight plaque and decay.

Periodontal Treatment

We believe in a prevention-first approach to gum disease. Visit us for your routine exams to catch signs of periodontal issues early and address them quickly and comfortably.

Comprehensive Exams

Our comprehensive exams evaluate every aspect of your oral health to detect issues early and ensure long-term well-being.

Dental Fillings

Our tooth-colored composite fillings restore damaged teeth and match your natural smile. We use modern, painless techniques.

Dental Crowns and Bridges

We use lifelike, durable crowns and bridges to restore damaged or missing teeth and help you smile with confidence again.

Root Canals

Our gentle root canal treatments relieve pain and save infected teeth while ensuring your comfort throughout the procedure.
